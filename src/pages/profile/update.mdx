---
title: Update profile
description: Guides for updating profile
---

To update your profile:

1. Go to [profile settings](https://platform.projectpandora.xyz/profile) page and choose the **General** tab.
2. Input new information.
3. Save by click **Update profile**.

To update your password:

1. Go to [profile settings](https://platform.projectpandora.xyz/profile) page and choose the **Password** tab.
2. Input current password, new password.
3. Press **Change password** to change new password.
