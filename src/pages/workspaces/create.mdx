title: Workspaces
description: Guide for creating workspaces
---

<PageDescription>

Workspaces is either a root domain or a subnet.

</PageDescription>

To create a new workspace:

1. Navigate to the workspace list in your organization and click **Add new** button (near the top of the page).

2. On the open popup, input name and domain / subnet. Ex: _Pandora - domain - projectpandora.xyz_.

3. Confirm creation with the **Save** button.
