## First steps

1. **Login / Signup**

   Visit [Pandora Dashboard](https://platform.projectpandora.xyz) and login with your existing account or create a new one.

2. **Create a new organization**

   An organization is a virtual space that stores all of your scan data and allows teammates to collaborate within.

3. **Add workspaces for your organization**

   A workspace is either a root domain or a subnet. To create one, click **Add new** button in the workspaces page.

4. **Start hunting!**

   From now on, you can create scan in different modes and configuration with the workspaces you just have created. Happy hunting!
