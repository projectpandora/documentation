---
title: Managing Access to Organizations
description: Guides for managing access to organizations
---

<PageDescription>

Pandora provides granular access management for organizations.

</PageDescription>

To manage access in your organization:

1. Click the **Settings** navigation in the top header.

2. On the Settings page, choose **Users** tab.

3. From there, you can see a list of users that currently has access to your organization. To edit their permissions, click the Edit icon. Note: _There must be at least one person who has admin privileges in your organization_.

4. To add a new user, click **Add new** button at the top and input their email. In the next section, choose permissions for this user and click **Add**.
