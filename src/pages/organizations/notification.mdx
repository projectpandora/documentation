---
title: Organization Notifications
description: Guides for managing notification for organizations
---

<PageDescription>

Pandora supports notifications via multiple channels.

</PageDescription>

To setup notifications in your organization:

1. Click the **Settings** navigation in the top header.

2. On the Settings page, choose **Notifications** tab.

3. Enable channels that you want to send notifications and input necessary configurations. You can send a test message by press **Check connection**.

4. In the **events** sections, tick to enable events that you want to get notifications.
